Pakistani Delights

  1. Pakora

    A deep-fried fritter made with a mixture of gram flour, spices, and vegetables.

  2. Samosa

    A crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat.

  3. Seekh Kebab

    Skewered and grilled minced meat kebabs, flavored with aromatic spices.

  4. Aloo Tikki

    Shallow-fried potato cutlets served with chutney and yogurt.

  5. Haleem

    A thick and hearty meat stew made with lentils, wheat, and spices.

  6. Papri Chaat

    A popular street food snack with crispy fried dough wafers, chickpeas, potatoes, and tangy chutneys.

  7. Chicken Tikka

    Marinated and grilled chicken pieces, bursting with flavors.

  8. Dahi Baray

    Soft lentil dumplings soaked in yogurt and garnished with spices and chutneys.

  9. Bread Pakora

    Spiced potato filling sandwiched between slices of bread and deep-fried.

  10. Samosa Chaat

    A delightful combination of crushed samosas topped with yogurt, chutneys, and spices.
