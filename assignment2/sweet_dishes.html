Sweet Dishes

  1. Gulab Jamun

    A soft and spongy milk-based dessert soaked in a sweet syrup, often flavored with rosewater.

  2. Kheer

    A rice pudding made with milk, rice, sugar, and flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts.

  3. Jalebi

    A deep-fried pretzel-shaped sweet made from wheat flour batter and soaked in sugar syrup.

  4. Gajar Ka Halwa

    A delicious and rich carrot-based dessert cooked with milk, ghee, and nuts.

  5. Falooda

    A refreshing dessert beverage with rose syrup, vermicelli, basil seeds, and ice cream.

  6. Shahi Tukda

    A royal dessert made with fried bread soaked in a sweetened milk sauce and garnished with nuts.

  7. Ras Malai

    Soft cottage cheese dumplings served in a creamy milk sauce, flavored with cardamom and saffron.

  8. Kulfi

    A traditional frozen dessert made with thickened milk, nuts, and various flavors.

  9. Phirni

    A creamy rice pudding flavored with saffron, cardamom, and garnished with nuts.

  10. Rabri

    A sweet, condensed milk-based dessert with a creamy texture and subtle flavors.
