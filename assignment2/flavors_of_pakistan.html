Flavors of Pakistan

  1. Biryani

    A fragrant rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, meat (such as chicken, mutton, or beef), and sometimes vegetables.

  2. Qourma

    A rich and flavorful meat stew cooked with a blend of spices and served with naan or rice.

  3. Karahi

    A savory meat or vegetable dish cooked in a thick, spicy tomato-based curry.

  4. Haleem

    A slow-cooked meat and lentil stew, rich in flavors and textures.

  5. Nihari

    A slow-cooked meat dish with a rich, spiced gravy, typically enjoyed with naan.

  6. Tikka Masala

    Grilled chicken or paneer cooked in a creamy and flavorful tomato-based sauce.

  7. Chicken Biryani

    A delicious variation of biryani with marinated chicken pieces layered with aromatic rice.

  8. Palak Paneer

    Soft cottage cheese cubes cooked in a creamy spinach sauce, packed with nutrients.

  9. Chapli Kebab

    Spiced ground meat patties made with a blend of herbs and served with naan.

  10. Daal Chawal

    A comfort food combination of lentils and rice, served with a dollop of ghee.
